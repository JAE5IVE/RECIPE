The Most Delicious Goat Meat Peppersoup

This Goat meat Peppersoup is not just any ordinary Peppersoup, it is a traditional Eastern Nigerian dish that is made with just parts from the goat head.
It is also known as Isi Ewu (goat head peppersoup or spiced goat head). It is one of Nigerian delicacies that is highly medicinal with aromatic
spices which can be eaten any time of year especially during periods of recuperation after illness and child birth. it can also be enjoyed as a comforting meal.

Recipe Information:

- Preparation time: 15 minutes

- Cook time: 35 minutes

- Servings: 4 plates

- Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients:

  • 1 goat head
  • 3 fresh chili pepper
  • 8 fresh tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon peppersoup seasoning
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 200ml/7floz palm-oil
  • 1 litre water
  • chopped wild mint
  • salt to taste
  • Dry grinded pepper
  • Seasoning - 4 Maggi/knorr cubes
  • Ehu seeds

Instructions:

  1. Scrape and singe the hairs off the head of the goat properly
  2. Wash the head thoroughly with a rough sponge or brush until it is clean
  3. Chop into small pieces with a sharp cleaver and rinse thoroughly
  4. Pour over the lemon juice and mix in. Leave to rest for 15 minutes
  5. Grind together the onions. garlic, chili pepper and tomatoes.
  6. Fry Ehu seeds (stirring constantly) till you can smell it and then peel off outer membrane and grind with a dry mill
  7. Place meat in a large pot and pour enough water to cover the contents of the pot. Add the grinded items (including the Ehu seeds) and seasoning
  8. Cover and cook for 20 minutes. Check and stir frequently
  9. Finally add the grinded pepper, salt, diced onions, palm-oil and wild mint to the pot.
  10. Simmer gently for 10-15 minutes until meat is tender, taste and adjust seasoning to your liking
  11. Serve in traditional earthenware dishes garnish with onion rings and chopped mint

Notes

  • Ehu seeds has a distinctive aroma so you will know when it is OK to take it off.
  • Goat head is a tough meat part so when cooking, you should us a pressure cooker if available.
  • some of the Ingredients such as ehu seeds are essential but can also be swapped with pepper
  • Even though it is called pepper soup, you still want to be able to taste and enjoy the recipe itself. Too much pepper can ruin it for you
